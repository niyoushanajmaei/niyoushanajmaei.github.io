About Me

I am a PhD student in the Partout team of LIX at École Polytechnique in France. I work on semantics of type theories with Ambroise Lafont.

Before this, I did my master's at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands. For my master's thesis I worked with Benedikt Ahrens on internal language of comprehension categories. You can find my master's thesis here.

I am interested in:

  • Type theories and their categorical semantics
  • Logic
  • Homotopy type theory and univalent foundations

I prefer They/Them pronouns, but She/Her works as well.
